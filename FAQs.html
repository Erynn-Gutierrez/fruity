FAQs

What is a cultural fruit box?

A cultural fruit box is a curated selection of fruits that represents a specific culture or country. It can include fruits that are commonly consumed in that culture or ones that hold cultural significance.

How do you select the fruits that go into a cultural fruit box?

We have a team of experts who carefully select and curate the fruits for each cultural fruit box. We consider factors such as the seasonality and availability of the fruits, as well as their cultural significance and popularity in the specific culture or country.

Can I customize my cultural fruit box?

Unfortunately, we do not offer customization for our cultural fruit boxes at this time. However, we do offer a variety of different cultural fruit boxes, so you can choose the one that best represents the culture or country you are interested in.

How long does it take for a cultural fruit box to be delivered?

Delivery times may vary depending on your location and shipping method. Typically, our standard shipping takes 3-5 business days, while expedited shipping takes 1-2 business days.
